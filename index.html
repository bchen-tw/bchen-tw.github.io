About me

Being someone with an electrical engineering and physics background, I was taught and trained to think logically, observe things from different aspects, and solve problems like an engineer. And that kind of affects my personality - I like to do things reasonably. I am self-disciplined, and I sometimes make up 'protocols' for myself to follow.
